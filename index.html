What Gradio offers

Everything you need to stay
on top of your academics.

Built specifically for Singapore students across polytechnics, universities, and ITE.

GPA Calculator

Enter your modules, credits, and grades — your GPA updates instantly. Organised by semester, with a clear cumulative view at all times.

  • Accurate grading for all major Singapore institutions
  • Semester-by-semester breakdown
  • What-If simulation mode
Comeback Tracker

If your GPA isn't where you want it to be, Gradio shows you the exact path forward — what you need each semester to hit your target.

  • Recovery feasibility analysis
  • Projected GPA trend visualisation
  • Semester-by-semester targets
Semester Planner

Map out future semesters before they happen. Balance your workload, flag high-stress periods, and plan strategically toward your goals.

  • Module difficulty and type tracking
  • Stress level estimation per semester
  • Workload balance detection
Export & Backup

Generate a clean, formatted PDF report of all your grades and GPA — far easier to reference than trawling through individual semester emails.

  • Professional PDF report export
  • JSON data backup & restore
  • All data stays on your device
How it works

Up and running in minutes.

No sign-up. No setup. Just open and go.

01
Select your institution

Choose from all major Singapore polytechnics, universities, and ITE. Your grading scale is configured automatically.

02
Add your modules

Enter your module names, credit units, and grades. Gradio calculates your GPA in real time as you type.

03
Explore and plan

Use the What-If simulator, Comeback Tracker, and Planner to understand your options and set clear targets.

04
Export your report

Download a clean PDF with your full academic record — a single document that consolidates everything, semester by semester.
